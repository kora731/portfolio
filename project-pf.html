PathFinder: Patient Board for Discharge Planning

Actionable Data Delivered in a Delightful Humane Way

Overview

Intro

Jan, 2018 - Current PathFinder is a patient board to facilitate discharge planning, presented on the TV while the care team huddles in the morning to help drive collaboration and proactive planning of patient discharge. PathFinder by Qventus is a key offering to customers to streamline patient flow to reduce length of stay and improve flow efficiency.

My Role

I‘m the lead designer to redesign patient board. From initial brainstorming to final specs, I worked along with the team of PM, engineers to deliver the product within 5 months, which became top sale products and received a lot of positive feedback from customers.

Problem Space

Target User Group: For care team, they need to improve efficiency and proactively address barriers / limited time for each patient / poor UX of EMR system

Business Impact: For hospital sites, they want to reduce LOS to lower cost

Product Strategy: Qventus need to ship products with stellar visuals, scalable configuration to win over competition

Design Challenge

How to redesign the patient board to enable proactive discharge planning and scalable to other use cases?
