Mobile App: Deliver Realtime Intervention to Patient Care Team

Right Data for the Right People at the Right Time

Overview

Intro

Mobile app by Qventus is a last-mile solution for frontline care team to be aware of context and also prepare for upcoming problems. It delivers real time nudges to help make better decisions, and support different roles to collaborate in a single thread.

My Role

I'm the lead designer on mobile app to launch multiple new features to reduce friction, and also came up with a redesign to increase the mobile app value to end users. For the design vision of nudge framework, I cooridated a design sprint to work with other stakeholders to come up with the roadmap to develop enhanced nudges.

Experience Goals

Delightful to engage users, helpful while not intrusive

Foster collaboration and positive mindset within the care team

Insightful and automated, push useful data to users rather than pull model

Design Challenge

User Problems:

1. Doctors and nurses cannot access data easily. They have to do heavy lifting to dig into the data and find out what happened. Locating and analyzing patient data is very time consuming and leads to frustration and burnout.

2. Hospitals have highly mobile environment, busy schedule and distributed teams. It's hard to coordinate different departments to solve problems together.

Solution Framework

1. Reduce Friction

2. Increase Value

  • Enable collaboration and decision making
  • Pushed real time and contextual data
  • Transparent outcome and help team stay motivated

3. HOOK Model (Design Sprint)
